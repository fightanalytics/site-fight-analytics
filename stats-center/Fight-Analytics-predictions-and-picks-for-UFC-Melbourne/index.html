Fight Analytics predictions and picks for UFC Melbourne

Nov 21, 2016 • by Fábio de Brito - CEO Fight Analytics

Share this:

Robert Whittaker vs. Derek Brunson

Tough fight for both Whittaker and Brunson. 5 rounds change it all. Luke Rockhold vs. Ronaldo Souza were the headliners for this event, so Whittaker and Brunson were supposed to go 3 rounds.

Anyhow, I will keep on my pick for this fight:

PICK:

WINNER: Robert Whittaker - UNDER / OVER: Under


- - -

Jake Matthews vs. Andrew Holbrook

Jake Matthews is a young tough prospect. He might have got a little bit to precipitate on his last appearance, but he remains as the favorite for this bout.

PICK:

WINNER: Jake Matthews


- - -

Kyle Noke vs. Omari Akhmedov

Omari replaces Dominique Steele who got injured. Noke and Akhmedov have been irregular, so it might be difficult to make a pick on each one of them will get this fight.

PICK:

UNDER / OVER: Over


- - -

Yusuke Kasuya vs. Alex Volkanovski

Both fighters tend to finish their bouts quick.

PICK:

UNDER / OVER: Over


- - -

Dan Kelly vs. Chris Camozzi

Kelly and Camozzi are going through different times. Kelly has been going up , even with his lost to Sam Alvey in november.

PICK:

WINNER: Dan Kelly


- - -

Damien Brown vs. Jon Tuck

Brown have finished 5 of his last 6 fights (3 submissions 2 KO/TKO, 1 decision).

Tuck is a good fighter but has been irregular (3v - 3d UFC).

PICK:

UNDER / OVER: Under


- - -

Richard Walsh vs. Jonathan Meunier

Both Richard and Meunier finish their fights quick.

PICK:

UNDER / OVER: Under


- - -

Ben Nguyen vs. Geane Herrera

Ben is more experienced and has been performing better in his last fights. I'm going with Over as well because of the match up of styles.

PICK:

WINNER: Ben Nguyen - UNDER / OVER: Over


- - -

Dan Hooker vs. Jason Knight

Both Hooker and Knight finish their fights quick.

PICK:

UNDER / OVER: Under


- - -

Yao Zhikui vs. Jenel Lausa

Both Yao and Jenel do not finish their fights quick.

PICK:

UNDER / OVER: Over


- - -

Seo Hee Ham vs. Danielle Taylor

Danielle has a more aggressive approach to the fight.

PICK:

WINNER: Danielle Taylor - UNDER / OVER: Over


- - -

Tyson Pedro vs. Khalil Rountree

Both Tyson and Khalil finish their fights quick.

PICK:

UNDER / OVER: Under
