Fight Analytics predictions and picks for The Ultimate Fighter 24 Finale

Nov 29, 2016 • by Fábio de Brito - CEO Fight Analytics

Share this:

Joseph Benavidez x Henry Cejudo

Very very difficult fight to predict. Both very strong athletes with great power on their hands. However based on their record is hard no to go with OVER.

PICK:

UNDER / OVER: Over


- - -

Jake Ellenberger x Jorge Masvidal

Jake performed better than Masvidal on his last fight.

PICK:

WINNER: Jake Ellenberger


- - -

Ion Cutelaba x Jared Cannonier

Strong fighters with very powerful strike game. I'm going with UNDER and guessing Cutelaba will get the win.

PICK:

WINNER: Ion Cutelaba - UNDER / OVER: Under


- - -

Sara McMann x Alexis Davis

The solid strike game from Sara Mcmann will play a part on this fight however I'm going with Alexis Davis experience and foot work.

PICK:

WINNER: Alexis Davis - UNDER / OVER: Over


- - -

Gray Maynard vs Ryan Hall

Fighter going through different phases. Ryan looks fresher, however I see Maynard experience and wrestling playing a part on this fight.

PICK:

WINNER: Gray Maynard - UNDER / OVER: Over


- - -

Kailin Curran VS Jamie Moyle

Kailin moves very well both offensive and defensive. Jamie Moyle has an excellent record from Invicta. I'm going with Kailin for her well posted strike game.

PICK:

WINNER: Kailin Curran - UNDER / OVER: Over


- - -

Josh Stansbury x Devin Clark

Both fighter tough fighters with good records from different leagues. However I don't see consistent enough from any side for this fight finishes quick.

PICK:

UNDER / OVER: Over


- - -

Elvis Mutapcic x Anthony Smith

I'm going with Anthony kicks and strength. Both fighters do not finish their bouts quick.

PICK:

WINNER: Anthony Smith - UNDER / OVER: Over


- - -

Dong 'Maestro' Kim x Brendan O'Reilly

Both Dong Kim and O'Reilly come from losses. O'REILLY can be precautious and got some solid punches from stand up and top positions. Under I’m guessing because I can see some holes on both fighter's games where both man can capitalized on it.

PICK:

WINNER: Brendan O'Reilly - UNDER / OVER: Under


- - -

Brandon Moreno x Ryan Benoit

I'm only sure it will be a great battle. Both young and well rounded prospects. My pick is Moreno tight and offensive grappling game.

PICK:

UNDER / OVER: Under


- - -


Rob Font x Matt Schnell

Another great fight to watch. Font and Perez are very serious when they mean to finish their business quick.

PICK:

UNDER / OVER: Under
